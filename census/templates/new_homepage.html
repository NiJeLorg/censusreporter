Housing Information Portal

Welcome to D3’s Housing Information Portal! Here, we provide data and resources about housing and property in Detroit, Southeast Michigan, and beyond. Scroll down to learn more about what we have available!

Geographic Area

Here, you can find data about housing trends for any geography in Michigan (census tracts, zip codes, cities, etc.). Don’t see a geography that works for you? You can also create your own!

Parcel ID or Address

Here, you can find up-to-date information about any property in Detroit. Data updates quarterly.
