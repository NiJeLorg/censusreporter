2017 State of the Detroit Child

The mission of State of the Detroit Child is to ensure the well-being of Detroit's kids. To do that, we place tremendous value on having reliable, timely data to drive decision-making and informed advocacy at every level from policy-makers to foundations to individual families. The best decisions can't be made if we donâ€™t know where kids are, what demographic shifts are underway, what populations are growing or shrinking, what the greatest needs are, and what trends are emerging that should inform our thinking.

To facilitate data access, Data Driven Detroit created the State of the Detroit Child tool, a comprehensive data view of Detroit children from birth to age 18 that also situates this population in the context of the rest of Detroit. The State of the Detroit Child tool is designed to provide dynamic information for policy-makers, educators, child advocates, and community stakeholders to guide current benchmarking and future decision-making.
