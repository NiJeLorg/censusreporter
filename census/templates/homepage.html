State of the Detroit Child

The goal of the State of the Detroit Child data platform is to provide reliable, timely data to improve the well-being of Detroit's kids by driving informed decision making and advocacy at every level, from policymakers, foundations and nonprofit organizations to individuals and families.

The State of the Detroit Child tool provides a comprehensive data view of Detroit children from birth to age 18 that situates this population in the context of the city at large. The information is regularly updated as new data are available, so changes and emerging trends can quickly be identified.
