Michigan Third Grade English Language Arts Proficiency

The table D3-ELA-Proficiency houses data on the following columns:

Columns Description
ELANumAssessed_Calc Total students taking ELA Assessment
ELATotalMet_Calc Number of students who met or exceeded expectations in ELA
ELATotalNotMet_Calc Number of students who did not meet expectations in ELA

Michigan Eighth Grade Math Proficiency

The table D3-Eighth-Grade-Math-Assessment houses data on the following columns:

Columns Description
numberasse Total students taking Math Assessment
totalmet Number of students who met or exceeded grade level standards in Math

Michigan High School Graduation Rate Table

The table D3-Graduation-Rates houses data on the following columns:

Columns Description
CohortCount Total number of students with the potential to graduate in the current shcool year
GradCnt Number of graduates in the current school year
GradRate Percentage of potential graduates to graduate in the current year

Michigan Student Mobility

The table D3-Student-Mobility houses data on the following columns:

Columns Description
s_count Number of public school students.
s_mobile Number of public school students either leaving or entering a new school during the school year.

Michigan Chronic Absenteeism

The table D3-Chronic-Absenteeism houses data on the following columns:

Columns Description
n_total Number of public school students.
n_chronabs Number of public school students missing 10% or more school days.

Michigan College Enrollment

The table D3-College-Enrollment houses data on the following columns:

Columns Description
TotGrad Number of public high school graduates who received a diploma during the high school graduation year.
TotEnrl Number of public high school graduates who have enrolled in college within 12 months of graduation.
