Michigan Third Grade English Language Arts Proficiency

The table D3-ELA-Proficiency houses data on the following columns:

Columns Description
ELANumAssessed_Calc Total students taking ELA Assessment
ELAProf Number of students who were proficient in ELA
ELAPartProf Number of students who were partially proficient in ELA
ELANotProf Number of students who were not proficient in ELA
ELATotalMet_Calc Number of students who met or exceeded expectations in ELA
ELANotMet Number of students who did not meet expectations in ELA
ELAAdv Number of students who were advanced in ELA
