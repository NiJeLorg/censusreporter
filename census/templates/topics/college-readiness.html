The table D3-College-Readiness houses data on the following columns:

Columns Description
AllSbjtNumAssessed Number of students who took the SAT Assessment in all subjects.
MathNumAssessed Number of students who took the Math SAT Assessment.
EBRWNumAssessed Number of students who took the Evidenced-Based Reading and Writing SAT Assessment.
AllSbjtNumReady Number of students who scored at or above college readiness proficiency on the SAT test in all subjects.
MathNumReady Number of students who scored at or above college readiness proficiency on the SAT test in Math.
EBRWNumReady Number of students who scored at or above college readiness proficiency on the SAT test in Evidenced-Based Reading and Writing.
FinalAllSbjtAveScore Average of all the SAT scores.
FinalMathAveScore Average of all the Math SAT scores.
FinalEWBRWAveScore Average of all the Evidenced-Based Reading and Writing SAT scores.
AllSbjtPctReady Percentage of students who scored at or above college readiness proficiency on the SAT test in all subjects.
MathPctReady Percentage of students who scored at or above college readiness proficiency on the SAT test in Math.
EBRWPctReady Percentage of students who scored at or above college readiness proficiency on the SAT test in Evidenced-Based Reading and Writing.
